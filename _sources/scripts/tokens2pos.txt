==========
tokens2pos
==========

Description
===========

::

    Usage: textkit tokens2pos [OPTIONS] [TOKENS]
    
      Tokenize words into their parts of speech. Output contains the word token
      followed by its part-of-speech tag, separated by the character specified
      by --sep.
    
    Options:
      -s, --sep TEXT  Separator between words in the output.  [default: ,]
      --help          Show this message and exit.
