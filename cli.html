Usage¶

textkit is composed of command-line tools that can be divided into four major categories: Tokenization, Transformation, Filtering, and Packaging. Documentation and examples for each tool are described on the following pages.

Tokenization¶

  • text2words
  • text2ngrams
  • text2punc
  • text2sentences
  • words2bigrams
  • words2ngrams

Transformation¶

  • tokens2lower
  • tokens2upper
  • tokens2stem
  • tokens2counts
  • tokens2pos
  • tokens2topbigrams

Filter¶

  • filterlengths
  • filterpunc
  • filterwords

Package¶

  • texts2json
  • tokens2json
  • tokens2text

Misc¶

  • download
  • nonewlines
  • showstops
